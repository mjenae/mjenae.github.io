Meeting and Collaborating

The Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program is dedicated to exposing 10th-11th grade girls to tech jobs across the country. Over the course of seven weeks, girls have the oppurunity to learn how to code while devloping relationships with other girls sharing the same interests.

State Farm Atlanta

A special thanks to State Farm Atlanta near Perimeter Center! The company was so kind to host our Girls Who Code program during the summer of 2017.

Learn more abount Laura Martinez

I'm Laura Martinez and I have a huge passion for creating things. I enjoy making my ideas come to life and love showing what I've created to others. While taking part in the Girls Who Code program, I enjoyed learning ways to express my ideas using coding as I had many opportunities to share them with wonderful girls. The program really allowed me to stretch myself and I'm extremely grateful for that.

Learn more about Madison Saunders

My name is Madison Saunders and I was blessed with the oppurtunity to participate in the Girls Who Code Program during the summer of 2017. The program led me to meeting Laura, who I then collaborated with on our final project and I've never been more appreciative. The program inspired me to wonder how I could integrate Computer Science with my desire to become an Aeronautical Engineer. Within the next year, I hope to showcase my 'combined ideas' through websites, like this, I will create. Who would've known I would discover a love for building websites from scratch!
